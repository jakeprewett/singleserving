California Wildfires

In late 2018, the Camp and Woolsey wildfires tore across California, destroying homes and talking lives in their path.

These images were taken in the first week of 2019, months after the fires were over.

Does it seem like there are more wildfires than in previous years? There are.

Climate change will probably worsen wildfires.

But wildfires can also make climate change worse.

Smoke from wildfires is a public health problem.

The fires have killed more people than the previously 3 worst wildfires combined.

The fires have destroyed more structures than the next 7 worst fires combined.

Look forward to more burned-over landscapes in California.

This single-serving website was created using photographs taken on a trip this past winter.
